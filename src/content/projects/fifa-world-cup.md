## FIFA World Cup Daily Panel Series

The highest-pressure project of Hisham's QMC tenure — a daily live show during the biggest sporting event Qatar has ever hosted, produced entirely without a support team.

### The Work
A 1-hour live panel discussion broadcast daily on Urdu Radio FM 107, structured in 4 segments. The show ran from approximately 2 weeks before the FIFA World Cup 2022 kicked off through the final match.

### My Role
- Sole producer AND host — no production team behind the show
- Researched daily talking points aligned with the tournament schedule
- Booked and managed guests: ex-club players, current coaches, sports news analysts, active club-level players
- Managed all live transitions between segments
