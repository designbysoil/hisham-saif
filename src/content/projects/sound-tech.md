## 'Sound-tech' — Technology Series

A passion project that brought tech literacy to Qatar's Urdu-speaking expatriate audience through accessible, fast-paced radio segments.

### The Work
A 15–20 minute program airing twice weekly on Urdu Radio FM 107. Each episode explored a different corner of consumer technology — from app reviews and software tutorials to cybersecurity awareness and emerging digital trends.

### My Role
- Independently researched every episode topic — no editorial team or content brief provided
- Wrote complete scripts for each broadcast
- Hosted all episodes live
- Collaborated with the station's sound engineer for music scoring and final editing
