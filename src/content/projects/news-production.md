## News Production & Live Event Coverage

The backbone of Hisham's time at QMC — a four-year commitment to daily live broadcasting that built deep skills in editorial research, live presentation, and field reporting.

### The Work
Daily newsroom duties on Urdu Radio FM 107, Qatar's national Urdu-language radio service. The role combined studio-based news presentation with solo field reporting assignments across Qatar.

### My Role
- Researched and translated news from Qatar News Agency (QNA) and local newspapers into Urdu
- Presented live news bulletins 4–5 days per week
- Conducted solo live field segments with interviews of government representatives
- Covered major national events as the station's primary field reporter

### Key Events Covered
- Summit for Peace 2021
- National Day flag-hoisting ceremony at the Pakistani Embassy
- Club-level cricket tournaments (live on-air commentary)
- Sports Day events
- Ajyal Film Festival 2022
