## The Morning Show & The Evening Show

The longest-format live broadcasts in Hisham's tenure — two separate prime-time shows that demanded sustained energy, editorial breadth, and audience management skills.

### The Work
Two separate live programs on Urdu Radio FM 107, each occupying a different prime-time slot:
- **The Morning Show** — 3 hours of live broadcasting
- **The Evening Show** — 3.5 hours of live broadcasting

### My Role
- Sole producer AND host for both shows
- Curated daily content across current affairs, weather updates, and cultural programming
- Conducted celebrity interviews (Ali Zafar, Shakti Kapoor, Irfan Khan, Imran Khan the actor)
- Managed live audience engagement via phone calls and messages
- Produced COVID-era public service announcements
- Organized weekly quizzes and prize contests
