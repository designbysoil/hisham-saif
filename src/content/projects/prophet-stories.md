## 'Prophet Stories' — Ramadan Series

A deeply researched narrative series that combined editorial rigor with storytelling craft, produced for Qatar's most-listened season of the year.

### The Work
A daily 30-minute pre-recorded show aired during Ramadan on Urdu Radio FM 107. Each episode narrated the story of a prophet mentioned in the Quran, drawing from a single well-reputed, authoritative reference book.

### My Role
- Conducted all research independently from the source text
- Wrote complete narrative scripts for each episode
- Performed all voice-over work
- Collaborated with the station's sound engineer for sound scoring and mastering
- Handled partial editing duties
